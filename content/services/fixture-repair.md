## Toilet Services

### Toilet Repair

A malfunctioning toilet isn't just annoyingâ€”it can waste thousands of gallons of water per month. A running toilet can lose 200+ gallons daily, adding significantly to your water bill while stressing your plumbing system.

**Common toilet problems we repair:**

**Running Toilet**
The most common toilet complaint. Causes include worn flappers, faulty fill valves, improper float adjustment, and sediment buildup. Most running toilets can be fixed in under an hour.

**Toilet Won't Flush Properly**
Weak flushes, incomplete flushes, and toilets that require multiple flushes to clear waste may have clogged rim jets, a faulty flapper, low water level, or a partial blockage.

**Toilet Keeps Clogging**
Frequent clogs can indicate problems beyond simple blockagesâ€”partial sewer line obstructions, buildup in the trap, or a toilet that's undersized for the household's needs.

**Toilet Leaking at Base**
Water pooling around the base indicates a failed wax ring seal between the toilet and the flange. This needs prompt repair to prevent subfloor damage and unhealthy conditions.

**Phantom Flushing**
When your toilet seems to flush by itself, it's actually slowly leaking from the tank to the bowl. This "phantom flush" wastes water and indicates a failing flapper or flush valve.

**Toilet Tank Sweating**
In humid conditions, cold water in the tank causes condensation on the outsideâ€”dripping onto your floor. Tank insulation kits or mixing valves can eliminate this problem.

**Loose or Rocking Toilet**
A toilet that moves when you sit isn't just uncomfortableâ€”it's breaking the wax seal and will eventually leak. We can reset and secure the toilet properly.

## Garbage Disposal Services

### Garbage Disposal Repair

Garbage disposals are workhorses that handle a lot of abuse, but they do require occasional maintenance and repair. Many problems have simple solutions.

**Common garbage disposal problems we repair:**

**Disposal Won't Turn On**
Often a reset button trip or electrical issue. If reset doesn't work, the problem may be the switch, wiring, or a failed motor.

**Humming But Not Spinning**
This usually indicates a jam. Something is stuck preventing the grinding plate from turning. We can clear jams and get your disposal working again.

**Leaking Disposal**
Leaks can occur at the sink flange, dishwasher connection, or discharge pipe. We identify the source and make appropriate repairs.

**Poor Grinding Performance**
Dull impellers or worn grinding components reduce effectiveness. Sometimes sharpening (running ice) helps; sometimes replacement is needed.

**Strange Noises**
Grinding noises, rattling, or metallic sounds often indicate foreign objects in the disposal or failing components.

**Disposal Drains Slowly**
Partial clogs in the disposal or drain pipe restrict water flow. Professional clearing restores normal drainage.

**Foul Odors**
Food buildup in the disposal creates unpleasant odors. We can clean the disposal and recommend maintenance practices.

### Garbage Disposal Installation

New disposals offer improved grinding power, quieter operation, and better reliability than older models. We install all major brands and can help you choose the right model.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does toilet repair cost?

Most toilet repairs range from $100-$250, depending on the problem. Flapper replacement is on the lower end; complete internal rebuilds are higher. Replacement toilets start around $300-$500 installed.

### Should I repair or replace my old faucet?

If your faucet is less than 10 years old and the repair cost is under 50% of replacement cost, repair usually makes sense. Older faucets or those requiring expensive parts are often better replaced.

### Can any faucet work with any sink?

Not necessarily. Faucets must match the number and spacing of holes in your sink. Single-hole, three-hole, and widespread configurations aren't interchangeable without modification.

### How long do garbage disposals last?

With proper use, garbage disposals last 8-12 years. Frequent jams, grinding non-food items, or running without water shortens lifespan.

### Why does my toilet keep running?

The most common cause is a worn flapper that doesn't seal properly. Fill valve problems, float misadjustment, and overflow tube issues can also cause running.

### Can you install a fixture I purchased?

Yes, we install customer-supplied fixtures. We recommend purchasing from a reputable retailer to ensure warranty coverage and authentic products.

### Do you offer warranties on fixtures?

We warranty our installation work. Fixtures carry the manufacturer's warranty, which we can help you register and understand.
